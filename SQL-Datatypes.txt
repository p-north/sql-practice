CHAR --- string(0-255) can store chars of fixed length.
VARCHAR - string(0-255) can store chars upto given length.
BLOB -- can store binary large object.
INT - regular integer type.
TINYINT -- integer(-128 to 127) small integer.
BIGINT -- very small values to larger values.
BIT -- can store x-bit values from 1 to 64.
FLOAT -- decimal data types.
DATE  -- date in format YYYY-MM-DD.
YEAR -- year in 4 digits ranging from 1901 to 2155.
